FEATURE

Comprehensive Subscription Management

SARKUS simplifies the entire subscription process by automating payments, reminders, and tracking for your club. Whether you're handling new sign-ups or renewals, our system ensures payments are processed smoothly and securely, reducing the need for manual intervention. Club administrators can view and manage all subscriptions from one dashboard, making it easy to track who has paid and who needs reminders.

FEATURE

Customizable Club Dashboard

With SARKUS's intuitive dashboard, club administrators have full control over their operations. You can adjust player information, modify subscription pricing, and even change membership types based on your club’s evolving needs. The dashboard also allows you to manage your club's bank details and automatically generates a payment link that can be shared with members via QR codes or your website for easy access.

FEATURE

Simple and Efficient User Subscription Form

SARKUS makes it incredibly easy for your members to complete their subscription with a streamlined, user-friendly form. Whether they are new members or renewing their subscription, they can complete the process in just a few clicks. For club administrators, all user data is automatically synced to the dashboard, allowing you to easily maintain, update, and manage member information, pricing, and subscription statuses. SARKUS keeps everything in one place, reducing manual input and simplifying updates whenever needed.

Automated Payments

Members pay their subscriptions easily through secure online payments, and the platform automates tracking and reminders, saving your club hours of manual work.

Increased Revenue

Time-Saving Management

From managing subscriptions to generating reports, SARKUS automates your administrative tasks, freeing up time to focus on your club’s development.
